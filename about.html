About Us

Discover our history, mission, and how we transform data into business growth.

Fiksu BI Solutions

A business intelligence consulting firm dedicated to empowering businesses through data-driven strategies. We specialize in transforming complex information into actionable insights, helping our clients achieve growth, efficiency, and a competitive edge.

Our comprehensive services ranging from data visualization and BI system setup to predictive analytics are tailored to meet the specific needs of each business we work with.

Mission

To make data accessible, understandable, and valuable for businesses of all sizes, transforming information into strategic growth opportunities

Vision

To be a trusted partner for businesses worldwide, providing intelligent data solutions that enhance decision-making and create long-term value.

Learn Our History


Fiksu BI Solutions was founded in 2022 by Mary K, who has years of experience in technology, finance, and data analytics. Recognizing the need for businesses to harness their data effectively, she launched the company to bridge the gap between complex information and business strategy. Initially, we focused on creating custom BI systems and dashboards that provided clear operational insights. As we grew, our services expanded to include predictive analytics and system integration, helping clients plan and optimize proactively.

Today, we partner with businesses worldwide across industries like finance, retail, healthcare, and technology, committed to transforming data into actionable insights that drive growth and success.
